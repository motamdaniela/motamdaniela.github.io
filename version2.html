read me ↘

I design and build digital experiences that live at the intersection of the web, games, and software systems. With a Bachelor's in Web Technologies and a Master's in Software Engineering currently underway, I bring together front-end craftsmanship, back-end architecture, and real-time interactivity. My work isn't just about making things work, it's about making them engaging, performant, and built to scale.

As a Game Dev educator and a student at the same time, this duality sets me appart and helps me stay sharp. I'm always searching for the "why" behind every line of logic. This teaching mindset permeates my development work, making me a clearer communicator, a better team player, and someone who thrives in collaborative, cross-disciplinary environments.

I'm currently deepening my expertise in software engineering, architecture, and development pipelines to build applications that are as robust as they are innovative. Whether I'm crafting a web platform, optimizing a game engine, or architecting a backend service, I approach every project with the same curiosity and rigor. I'm always looking for challenges that push me to grow and create something truly memorable.
